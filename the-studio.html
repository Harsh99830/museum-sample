Our Story

Born from the
Chaos of Color

The Studio began in 2018 as a single room, a few brushes, and an obsession with the unseen. What started as a private practice grew into a living, breathing creative space — a place where commissioned works are born and experimental pieces dare to exist.

We believe art is not decoration. It is documentation — of an era, an emotion, a fleeting second of human consciousness captured before it dissolves back into the ether.

Every piece that leaves this studio carries a fragment of that belief.

"
Art is not what you see, but what you make others see. Every canvas is a mirror — held up not to the world, but to the soul behind the eyes that dares to look.
— The Studio, Jaipur
The Process
01

Conception

Every work begins with silence. We sit with the idea — sometimes for days — before a single stroke is made. The mind must be emptied before it can be filled with something true.

02

Execution

Raw materials meet raw emotion. Oils, acrylics, mixed media — the medium is chosen not for aesthetics but for honesty. What does this feeling demand to be expressed in?

03

Refinement

The piece sits. We return to it with fresh eyes — sometimes after a week, sometimes a month. Layers are added or taken away until the work breathes on its own.
